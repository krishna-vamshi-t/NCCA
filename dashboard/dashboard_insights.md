# Netflix Churn Dashboard Insights

## Overview
This dashboard analyzes customer churn across 8,940 Netflix subscribers.
Overall churn rate stands at 14.44%.

## Page 1 - Executive Summary
- 14.44% overall churn rate
- Basic plan has the highest churn at 17.1%
- Premium plan has the lowest churn at 9.8%
- Brazil and Mexico are the highest churn countries

## Page 2 - Customer Analysis
- Churn is fairly equal across genders
- New customers (0-6 months) churn more than long-tenure customers
- High Risk segment churns at 27.3% vs Loyal at only 6.1%

## Page 3 - Behavior Analysis
- Churned customers watch 2 fewer hours per month on average
- Churned customers have higher payment failures
- Churned customers raise more support tickets
- Lower recommendation clicks correlate with higher churn

## Page 4 - Retention Insights
- 68.48% of customers are flagged as high risk
- Auto-renew disabled customers churn significantly more
- Basic plan customers with payment failures are highest priority to retain

## Key Recommendations
1. Target Basic plan users with payment failures for proactive outreach
2. Offer loyalty rewards to customers approaching 12 month tenure
3. Re-engage inactive users (15+ days) before they churn
4. Push auto-renew enablement campaigns in Brazil and Mexico
5. Improve onboarding for new customers in first 6 months
