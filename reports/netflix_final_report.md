## What This Project Did
Analyzed 10,000 Netflix customers to find who churns, why, and how to retain them.
Built a full Python pipeline with cleaning, feature engineering, segmentation and metrics.

## Numbers
- 14.44% overall churn rate
- 8,940 clean records
- 25 features after engineering
- 5 customer segments
- 25 SQL queries

## Biggest Findings
- Basic plan churns at 17.1%, Premium at 9.8%
- High Risk segment churns at 27.3%, Loyal at 6.1%
- Payment failures and inactivity are strongest churn signals
- Auto-renew disabled customers churn nearly double the rate
- Brazil and Mexico are highest risk countries

## Recommendations
- Target Basic plan users with payment issues first
- Re-engage users inactive for 15+ days
- Push auto-renew in high churn countries
- Reward loyalty at 6, 12, 24 month milestones
